Introduction

Dr. Salman Sajid Skin Institute is a state-of-the-art skin center, committed to serving its esteemed clients in a serene and professional environment. It is one of the ﬁrst laser clinics in Pakistan and the ﬁrst to introduce Nd:YAG laser into the Pakistani community. It has continued to keep its reputation for innovation by constantly adding the newest lasers into the clinic. These include Picoway, Ulterapy, RegeneraActiva, Thermi RF, Soprano Ice, Dermapen 4, Coolsculpting, Thermage, Revlite.These lasers are internationally recognized lasers that Dr. Salman Sajid Skin Institute can boast of in their environment. High professional standards are strictly maintained in the clinic, particularly in relation to sterilization and product quality.

We respect your privacy and with our private waiting area we provide you the comfort to resolve all your skin and body issues. All treatments are specially catered and designed for an individual under the guidance and care of Dr. Tasneem Nakhoda and competent team.

Dr. Salman Sajid-Senior Dermatologist

Dr. Salman Sajid is a Board Certiﬁed Dermatologist involved in a private practice in Karachi, Pakistan. He has over 20 years’ experience with training in Pakistan and the USA. He is renowned for her expertise in lasers, making her a leader in the ﬁeld of aesthetic dermatology. He has served as a consultant/speaker for many laser and cosmeceutical companies and has published papers in various journals.

He is a Dow graduate of 1987 and completed her MCPS in 1992. The same year He passed commission and joined Jinnah Post Graduate Medical Center where he trained MCPS students in dermatology, read papers and gave her FCPS part 1. He left the department to join Prof. Faiz in the plastic surgery department to complete her thesis in skin cancer. Her interest in pediatrics led her to join Dr. Amy Paller, an authority in dermatology and the President of the Society of Pediatric Dermatology based in Chicago. He also worked with Dr. Montalvo, a Senior Cosmetic Dermatosurgeon at the Northwestern University.

On her return from USA in 1999, she opened her own state of the art laser clinic named 'Lasersoft- the Skin Clinic' with both medical and aesthetic lasers. It is her constant pursuit of knowledge and hunger for innovative techniques, that has allowed her to introduce PRP and Fractional Radiofrequency in Pakistan.
